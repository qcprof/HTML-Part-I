Fixed Asset Depreciation

Depreciation Methods

For GAAP purposes, there are several different acceptable depreciation methods. We will be discussing each technique in detail.

The three techniques most commonly used are:

  1. Straight Line
  2. Sum of Years Digit
  3. Declining Balance
    • 150% Declining Balance
    • Double (200%) Declining Balance

Cost $100,000
Salvage Value $20,000
